About

What is EcoSort?

EcoSort is a mobile application designed to help you categorize your garbage and dispose them properly based on their properties. It allows you to detect the type of garbage you currently have by scanning it with your phone's camera. EcoSort also provides several articles about different types of garbage to give you more insights about them.

Features

  • Machine Learning based Garbage Detection

  • Informative Articles about Different Types of Garbage
